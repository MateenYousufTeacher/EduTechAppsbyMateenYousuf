NEP 2020 · FLN Priority

Foundational Literacy & Numeracy

NEP 2020 identifies FLN as the highest national priority in school education. Every child must achieve foundational reading, writing, and arithmetic skills before advancing to higher grades. This app directly supports this mandate through adaptive, personalized learning.

NCF Foundational Stage 2022

Playful & Activity-Based Learning

NCF 2022 emphasizes phonological awareness, number sense, and conceptual understanding through engaging, activity-based experiences. Every module in this app is designed following these NCF principles.
